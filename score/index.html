Score Caps — Critical Signal Override

Certain signals are severe enough to limit the total score regardless of other metrics. A token cannot be considered trustworthy if one fundamental risk is extreme.

  • 🚨 Top-20 wallets control ≥95% of supply → Score capped at 35 (CRITICAL) — creator effectively controls supply
  • 🚨 Top-20 wallets control ≥80% of supply → Score capped at 49 (HIGH RISK)
  • ⚠️ Top-20 wallets control ≥60% of supply → Score capped at 65 (PARTIAL RISK)
  • ⚠️ Holder distribution unavailable (high-activity tokens) → Score capped at 70 — concentration risk unverifiable
  • 🚨 Mint authority still active → Score capped at 60
  • ⚠️ Freeze authority still active → Score capped at 70
  • ⚠️ Mutable metadata (creator can change name / logo / URI) → Score capped at 85 — phishing vector
  • 🚨 Dangerous Token-2022 extensions detected → Score capped at 40
  • 🚨 Honeypot detected → Score capped at 15 — selling appears impossible
  • ⚠️ Suspicious sell pattern → Score capped at 50 — elevated failed sell rate
  • 🚨 Token or pool less than 48 hours old → Score capped at 60 — LP lock and creator intent unverifiable
  • ⚠️ 24h Vol/MCAP ratio >200% → Score capped at 65 — abnormal volume relative to market cap
  • 🚨 24h Vol/MCAP ratio >500% → Score capped at 49 — likely coordinated pump or wash trading
  • 🚨 24h Vol/MCAP ratio >1000% → Score capped at 35 (CRITICAL) — blatant manipulation, 10× mcap volume
  • ⚠️ LP confirmed unlocked → −5 pts penalty — creator can remove liquidity at any time
  • ⚠️ Liquidity data unavailable (DexScreener fetch failed) → Score capped at 50 — context unverifiable, manipulation cannot be ruled out
  • 🚨 3+ critical signals firing simultaneously → −10 pts compounding penalty — beyond reasonable doubt
