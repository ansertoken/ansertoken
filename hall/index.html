How this record works

01
Entry criteria
A token must meet all three: score ≤49 (HIGH RISK or CRITICAL), liquidity ≥$5,000 (real exposure possible), and at least one hard flag — mint active, honeypot detected, dangerous Token-2022 extension, Vol/MCAP >500%, or top-20 concentration ≥80%. The entry is created before any collapse.
02
Outcome tracked
The token's liquidity is monitored automatically. When a collapse is confirmed on-chain — liquidity below $500 or under 20% of the initial level (80%+ drained) — the rug date and days-to-collapse are recorded. Fast collapses are confirmed immediately when the pattern is unambiguous. Slow or ambiguous cases require at least 24h without recovery before confirmation. Pending = still being watched.
03
Permanent record
Entries added under current criteria are permanent. This record reflects tokens flagged from May 2026 onward under scoring version v1.0. The goose does not edit history.
04
Dispute policy
Every entry is based on public on-chain data at the time of audit. If you believe an entry contains a factual error — wrong address, incorrect flag, data fetch error — contact @theanserapp or t.me/theanser with the contract address and specific correction. Disputes are reviewed within 7 days. Factual errors are corrected and noted. Entries are not removed because a token recovered — the audit was a snapshot, not a verdict on the future.

This record contains on-chain signals only — timestamps, scores, and verifiable blockchain data. No entry implies legal fraud or criminal intent. Every flag is a reading of public on-chain state, not an accusation. Verify independently before acting on any information here.
